How Much Does AI Cost for UAE Businesses? (Real Numbers)

Everyone talks about AI. Nobody talks about what it actually costs.

Here's a realistic breakdown for UAE businesses in 2026.

Free (Yes, Really Free)

Many AI tools have generous free tiers:

  • ChatGPT Free — Writing emails, drafting content, basic analysis
  • Google Gemini — Document analysis, data extraction
  • Claude Free — Longer documents, coding help
  • Canva AI — Image generation for social media

For a solo business owner, free tools might be enough.

Low Cost (AED 100-500/month)

  • ChatGPT Plus — AED 95/month. Better models, more usage.
  • Claude Pro — AED 95/month. Good for analysis and writing.
  • Notion AI — AED 45/month. AI writing in your documents.
  • Zapier AI — AED 130/month. Connect apps without coding.

Medium Investment (AED 2,000-10,000 one-time)

Custom automation for one department:

  • ERPNext implementation (self-hosted) — AED 3,000-8,000
  • Custom dashboard — AED 2,000-5,000
  • Process automation — AED 1,500-4,000 per process

Full Implementation (AED 15,000-50,000)

End-to-end business transformation with custom systems, AI agents, and team training. This is for companies with 10+ employees where automation pays for itself in months.

Rule of thumb: If the automation saves 20+ hours per week, it pays for itself in 3 months or less. Everything else is just exploring.
