AI Readiness Guide for UAE Businesses: Is Your Company Ready?

AI is everywhere. But is your business actually ready for it?

Most UAE companies rush to "do AI" without the basics in place. Here's how to check if you're truly ready.

Question 1: Do You Have Clean Data?

AI runs on data. If your customer data is in five different spreadsheets with different formats, AI won't help — it will make the mess faster.

Ready if: Your data is in one place, clean, and consistent.

Not ready if: You still have paper records or data in three places that don't match.

Question 2: Do You Have a Clear Problem?

Don't start with "we need AI." Start with "we spend 10 hours a week on this task."

AI is a tool, not a goal. The goal is saving time, reducing errors, or serving customers better.

Ready if: You can name one specific task you want to automate.

Question 3: Can Your Team Use It?

The best AI tool is useless if your team can't or won't use it. Check if they have basic digital literacy and aren't afraid of technology.

Ready if: Your team already uses computers daily for their core work.

Question 4: Do You Have Buy-In?

AI projects fail when the CEO wants it but the team doesn't. Or the manager wants it but the CEO won't pay.

Everyone needs to agree: this is a priority, not a side project.

Question 5: Are You Ready for Change?

AI changes how work gets done. Some roles will shift. Some tasks will disappear. Are you prepared to manage that?

Scoring:

5 "ready" answers: You're ready. Start now.

3-4 "ready" answers: Almost there. Fix the gaps first.

0-2 "ready" answers: Focus on basics before AI.
