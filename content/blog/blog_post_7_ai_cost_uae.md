# How Much Does AI Implementation Cost in UAE? (Honest Answer)

The honest answer: it depends on what you're automating.

## The Range

| Business Size | What You're Automating | Cost Range |
|---------------|----------------------|------------|
| Small (5-20 people) | One workflow (invoicing, reporting) | AED 5,000 - 15,000 |
| Small (5-20 people) | Multiple workflows | AED 15,000 - 40,000 |
| Medium (20-100 people) | Full system (ERP + automation) | AED 40,000 - 150,000 |
| Medium (20-100 people) | Custom AI solution | AED 100,000 - 300,000 |
| Enterprise (100+) | Enterprise transformation | AED 300,000+ |

## Why the Range Is So Wide

A small trading company automating invoice processing is very different from a 200-person company building a custom AI platform.

The cost depends on:
- How many workflows you're automating
- How messy your current data is
- Whether you need custom development or off-the-shelf
- How many people need training

## The Trap to Avoid

Don't pay AED 200,000 for an AI tool when your data is in Excel.

Fix the data first. Then add AI. That's the difference between AED 20,000 that works and AED 200,000 that doesn't.
