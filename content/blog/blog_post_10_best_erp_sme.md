# Best ERP for Small Business in UAE (2026 Guide)

Small businesses in UAE have different ERP needs than enterprises.

Here's what to look for — and what to avoid.

## What Small Businesses Need

1. **Simple** — Your team shouldn't need a PhD to use it
2. **Affordable** — Under AED 500/month
3. **Scalable** — Grows with you from 5 to 50 people
4. **Customizable** — Fits your specific workflow
5. **Supported** — Someone to call when it breaks

## Top ERP Options for UAE SMEs

### ERPNext (Best Overall)
- Price: Free (open source) + implementation cost
- Best for: Trading, services, manufacturing
- UAE support: Yes (local partners)
- Learning curve: Medium

### Zoho ERP
- Price: AED 50-200/user/month
- Best for: General business, CRM + ERP combo
- UAE support: Yes
- Learning curve: Low

### Odoo
- Price: Free (community) + hosting
- Best for: Customizable, modular
- UAE support: Limited
- Learning curve: High

### Tally
- Price: AED 100-300/month
- Best for: Accounting-focused
- UAE support: Yes
- Learning curve: Low

## Our Recommendation

For most UAE small businesses, ERPNext is the best choice because:
- It's free (no license fees)

- It's customizable to your workflow
- Local partners (like Ailutions) provide support
