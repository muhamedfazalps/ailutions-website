# ERPNext Implementation in UAE: What Nobody Tells You

ERPNext is free. Open source. Powerful. And if you implement it wrong, it will cost you more than any paid software.

We've fixed 12 broken ERPNext implementations in the UAE. Here's what goes wrong — and how to avoid it.

## Mistake 1: Copying Your Old System

The biggest mistake? "Make ERPNext work exactly like our Excel sheets."

That defeats the purpose. ERPNext is a chance to fix broken processes, not digitize them.

**What to do instead:** Map your actual workflow first. Then configure ERPNext to match — not your spreadsheets.

## Mistake 2: Customizing Everything on Day One

"We need 47 custom fields and 12 custom reports before we go live."

No, you don't. Start with the standard modules. Use them for 30 days. Then you'll know exactly what you actually need.

**What to do instead:** Go live with standard ERPNext. Customize only after you've used it for a month.

## Mistake 3: No Training After Go-Live

"We trained the team once during implementation."

That's like teaching someone to drive once and expecting them to handle Dubai traffic forever.

**What to do instead:** Plan for 3 months of ongoing support. Weekly check-ins. Quick fixes. Help when people get stuck.

## Mistake 4: Choosing the Wrong Implementer

A cheap implementer who doesn't understand your business will cost you more than an expensive one who does.

**What to do instead:** Ask your implementer: "Have you worked in my industry before?" If the answer is no, keep looking.
