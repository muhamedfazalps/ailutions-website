# Is Your UAE Business Ready for AI? (Free Assessment Guide)

Most UAE businesses want AI. Few are ready for it.

Here's how to know if you're ready — and what to do if you're not.

## What "AI Ready" Means

AI ready doesn't mean "we bought ChatGPT for the team."

It means:
- Your data is organized in one place
- Your processes are documented
- Your team spends 40%+ of time on repetitive tasks
- You can measure the cost of manual errors
- You have clean, structured data

If you can't check those boxes, you're not ready for AI. You're ready for systems first.

## The 5-Question Test

Answer these honestly:

1. **Where does your data live?** One system? Or scattered across email, Excel, WhatsApp, and paper?

2. **Can you describe your core process in 10 steps or less?** If not, it's not documented.

3. **What happens when someone goes on leave?** Does the work stop? Or does the system handle it?

4. **How many hours per week does your team spend on data entry?** If it's more than 5, that's automation territory.

5. **Can you tell me your monthly revenue per employee?** If you can't calculate this, you don't have visibility.

## Scoring

- 5/5 yes: You're AI ready. Let's talk.
- 3-4 yes: You're almost there. Fix the gaps first.
- 1-2 yes: You need systems before AI.
- 0 yes: Start with a workflow audit.
